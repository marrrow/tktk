Understanding Sound Engagement Metrics

To help you make informed sound choices, WouldLiker uses these key engagement metrics:

  • Track Engagement: Calculated as (Likes + Shares + Comments + Favorites) / Views for videos using the track. This reflects overall audience interaction.
  • SoundOn Engagement: Based on a track's percentile ranking against all tracks with video views > 5k. This indicates relative performance within a competitive set. Ratings are:
    • Excellent: Top 10%
    • Great: Top 20%
    • Good: Top 30%
    • OK: Top 50%
    • Fair: Bottom 50%

WouldLiker's SoundOn Score™ algorithm intelligently combines these metrics to provide a predictive assessment of viral sound potential.
